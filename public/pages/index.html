Island Storytellers

The Island Storytellers is a friendly and open-invitation storytelling group that meets on the second Wednesday of every month alternating mornings (11-1; Town Choice café, Newport, April/June/Oct/Dec) and evenings (7-9, Yelfs Hotel, Ryde, May/July/Sept/Nov) for a casual story circle somewhere on the Isle of Wight. Tellers and listeners alike, all welcome. Stories told, not read, and no longer than ~5 -8 minutes each, unless by arrangement. NEXT STORY CIRCLE — December ?? ...
