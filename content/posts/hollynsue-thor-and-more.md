In the beginning there was only fire, ice and the yawning void of Ginnungagap.

From this came the world of the Norse gods - all-seeing Odin, enchanting Freya, thunderous Thor and the trickster Loki.

Step into this world of gods and giants with storytellers Sue Bailey and Holly Medland for an exciting and unexpected journey though the ancient Norse myths.

Cowes Combined Services and Social Club, Saturday, March 22nd, 2025. Doors open 6:30pm for 7pm start. Tickets, priced £8, available [here](https://www.ticketsource.co.uk/storytelling/thor-and-more-tales-from-the-land-of-ice-and-fire/e-lkzxrz).
