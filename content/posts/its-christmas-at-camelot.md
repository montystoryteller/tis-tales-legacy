**BOTH SHOWS CANCELLED — UNFORTUNATELY, WE HAD TO CANCEL THESE SHOWS DUE TO INJURY...**

**It's Christmas time at Camelot, as King Arthur sits waiting, for a tale of great chivalry, and derring do.**

_As he waits, the harpist plays, the singer sings, and the storyteller tells a tale or two..._

A new show from Island Based trio Stories'n'Harp, featuring storyteller Tony "Monty" Hirst, harpist Theresa Ellis, and singer Jenni Charity.

- _Monkton Arts, Wednesday, November 20th, 2024, 7pm._ CANCELLED
- _Medina Bookshop, Cowes, Monday, December 9th, 2024, 7pm._ CANCELLED

{{< figure src="/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/TH%5Fstoriesharp%5Fwinter24.png" alt="" caption="" >}}

_Since first teaming up at the end of 2023 for a harp accompanied telling of some ancient Welsh folk-tales, Tony "Monty" Hirst and Theresa Ellis were joined by singer Jenni Charity mid-way through 2024, to form Stories'n'Harp. A preview show of "Mediaeval Tales (WIP)" was followed by two different, and compelling, performances at Ventnor Fringe under the banner of "Tales of Britain Ireland"._

_With a growing repertoire of stories, music and song, each complementing the other, Stories'n'Harp weave magical landscapes capable of transporting audiences to those imagined times and places that always have been, never were, and are ever known of, as: Once..._
