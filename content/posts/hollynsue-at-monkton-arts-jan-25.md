As the new year begins, 'Tis Tales tellers Holy and Sue are taking a set of monkish tales — **Monk Tales: bad habits and Good Resolutions** — to [Monkton Arts](https://monktonarts.co.uk/), Ryde, on Thursday 16th January 2025, 6.30pm. Tickets priced £7 available [here](https://www.ticketsource.co.uk/somewhen-storytelling/monk-tales-bad-habits-and-good-resolutions/e-aekorz).

_**Step into a world where ancient wisdom meets timeless humour.**_

_In this set of traditional folktales, Sue and Holly will weave together stories of monks' journeys through temptation, redemption and spiritual growth. From humorous mishaps to moments of profound insight, these stories explore the age-old struggle between the divine and the wretched._
