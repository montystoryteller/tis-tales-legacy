On **Monday, October 17th, 2022, 7.30-9.30pm**, the Island Storytellers storytelling circle are promoting a special event at the **Ventnor Arts Club**, a one-night only performance event by singer and teller of the tallest of tales, **Keith Donnelly**.

Tickets, priced £6 + booking fee, are available from the [Ventnor Arts Club](http://www.ventnorartsclub.com/) or via [online ticket sales here](https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/an-evening-with-keith-donnelly-tickets-423935381137).

* * *

_The Island Storytellers meet once a month or so, typically on the third Tuesday of the month, typically in a pub somewhere on the Island, for an informal hour or two of round table storytelling. Tellers new and old, as well as listeners old and new, are always most welcome. Check out the [Island Storytellers Facebook group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/islandstorytellers), or the [Island Storytellers page](/island-storytellers/) here on the \`Tis Tales website, for regular updates about where the group is meeting next..._
