As part of Ventnor Fringe, 'Tis Tales teller Monty will be performing two shows as part of Stories'n'Harp, along with harpist Theresa Ellis and singer Jenni Charity.

So why not make a date in your diary for a pre-show lunch at the [Better Days Cafe](https://thebetterdayscafe.co.uk/), then a matinée of tales...

And with different tales each day, there's no reason not to come twice:-)

Tues, July 23rd, 2-3pmStories’n’Harp: Tales of Britain and Ireland (Wales & Ireland)Ingrams Yard[Tickets](https://ventnorexchange.littleboxoffice.com/events/75849)Weds, July 24th, 2-3pmStories’n’Harp: Tales of Britain and Ireland (England & Scotland)Ingrams Yard[Tickets](https://ventnorexchange.littleboxoffice.com/events/75850)
