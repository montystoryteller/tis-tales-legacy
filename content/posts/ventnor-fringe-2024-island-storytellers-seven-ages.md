With Ventnor Fringe week upon us, don't forget that the Island Storytellers' "gang show" will be hitting Ventnor Library this Wednesday, July 24th, 2024, 7-9.30pm. **[Tickets available here.](https://ventnorexchange.littleboxoffice.com/events/76550)**

Island Storytellers' is an informal story circle that meets once a month, somewhere on the Island, to swap tales and share stories. For more details, see the [Island Storytellers' Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/groups/islandstorytellers/). (Also see [here](/island-storytellers/).)
