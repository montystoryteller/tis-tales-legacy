As part of the Island-based [Somwhen Storytelling](https://somewhen.org.uk/) initiative, _'Tis Tales_ will be appearing as one of several _Somewhen_ affiliated events at Ventnor Fringe.

Search for _somewhen storytelling_ on the [Ventnor Fringe website](https://vfringe.co.uk/whats-on/).

Tickets for all our events are already on sale from the Ventnor Fringe Box Office. **_Please help spread the word..._**

- [DANIEL MORDEN — DARK TALES FROM THE WOODS](https://vfringe.co.uk/events/dark-tales-from-the-woods/) _(performance storytelling from internationally renowned, award winning Welsh storyteller, Daniel Mordern)_
- ['TIS TALES — HERE BE GIANTS](https://vfringe.co.uk/events/dark-tales-from-the-woods/) _(performance storytelling from Island based storytelling trio 'Tis Tales...)_
- [ISLAND STORYTELLERS — GREEN](https://vfringe.co.uk/events/green/) _(Island Storytellers' storytelling gang show)_
- [TONY "MONTY" HIRST — UNFORESEEN CONSEQUENCES](https://vfringe.co.uk/events/dark-tales-from-the-woods/) _(performance storytelling that doesn't quite fit the two song folk night/open mic slot format )_
- [SUE BAILEY — ONE SOCK - A STORYTELLING RIDDLE FILLED SOCK HUNT](https://vfringe.co.uk/events/one-sock-a-storytelling-riddle-filled-sock-hunt/) _(ever popular, family friendly story walks with Sue)_

**If you like storytelling (stories as traditionally told (NOT read), or are just intrigued as to how stories might not just be for children, please try to support these events. Any profits generated will go back into the pot to help us bring more professional storytellers to the Island.**
