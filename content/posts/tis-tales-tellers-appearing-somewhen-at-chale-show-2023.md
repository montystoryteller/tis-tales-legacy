With Ventnor Fringe over for another year, there's no stopping the stories this summer... Upcoming this weekend, all three 'Tis Tales tellers will be popping up in the _[Somewhenever Story Tent](https://thechaleshow.co.uk/storytent/)_ at Chale Show.

{{< figure src="/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/image-4.png" alt="" caption="" >}}

There's a full programme of tales and other activities across both days (Saturday and Sunday), with 20 minutes of tales on the hour each day, from 11am to 4pm inclusive.

{{< figure src="/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/image-5.png" alt="" caption="" >}}

_As part of the Somewhen Storytelling project, we are looking to raise awareness of storytelling as oral culture — tales told, not read — across the island, and across ages. Whilst the Chale Show story tent activities on the half hour are intended as family friendly events, many of the stories told on the hour are likely to be appropriate for all ages..._
