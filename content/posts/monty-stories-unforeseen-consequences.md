Taking some tales off the island, show-wise, for the first time, Monty will be doing a work-in-progress / warm-up telling of **Unforeseen Consequences** at Better Days Cafe, Ventnor on Friday, May 9th, doors at 7pm (and not before), bring your own bottle/booze/bag of (quiet) crisps, before taking it off to Chichester and Tunbridge Wells Fringe Festivals.

> A young girl and her brother are given a secret that brings a community together, before a King's purchase from a traveling tinker ruins his banqueting table. A family heirloom and a kindly deed bring other stories to mind, set against a world where there was too much time.

**Chichester Fringe, Saturday, 31 May 2025,** _**19:00 – 20:00**_ — _New Park Studio, New Park Rd, Chichester PO19 7XY, UK_ **[TICKETS AVAILABLE HERE](https://www.chichesterfringe.co.uk/event-details-registration/unforeseen-consequences)**.

**Tunbridge Wells Fringe, July 5, 2025 5.00pm**, _Grosvenor & Hilbert Park HUB_ **[TICKETS AVAILABLE HERE](https://twfringe.com/events/unexpected-consequences/)**
