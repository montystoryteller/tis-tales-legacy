We're delighted to announce that on the weekend of Sat/Sun August 3rd-th, 2024, we have the wonderful [Nell Phoenix](http://nellphoenix.co.uk/) back on the Island, with a couple of short all-comers sets in our story tent at Chale Show, and full adult audience storytelling set at [CHOYD](https://www.choyd.org.uk/) (Yarmouth Community Hall) on Saturday, August 4th, 2024, 7pm.

So if you're wondering what to do about getting your next cultural fix as Ventnor Fringe comes to a close, now you know....

Tickets available here: [https://www.ticketsource.co.uk/somewhen-storytelling](https://www.ticketsource.co.uk/somewhen-storytelling)
