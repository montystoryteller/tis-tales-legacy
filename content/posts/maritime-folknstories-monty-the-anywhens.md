Not that you should ever need an excuse to go into [The Star](https://ryde.cafe/) in Ryde, if you do happen to have nothing better to do next Thursday, September 25th, 2025, from 7pm, Monty will be there taking turns telling tales between songs from The AnyWhens, aka Glenn Koppany and Sue Cain.

Entry is _free_, the beer is good, and so is the coffee, and the pizzas always hit the spot, so why wouldn't you want to make a night of it...
