It's getting to that time of year when this word, and that world, collide. It's getting to that time of year when stories of a particular flavour call out to be told...

For Halloween this year, 'Tis Tale teller Holly is teaming up with [Merl Fluin](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100009945919321&__cft__[0]=AZVjIhbtApaeu_LjG3EkFigyCBrlE_jWXar1b95SE7bC1wBQlZ2uPT0qk9Oo2B3LOrrWCZmjRY_N2BJ1ri8OcrS81F8Smlr2WFOLqlvI0pP3zQlxW5XR0NKBIPO6NkkAd9E_JPatuVSwBEBDOyPLnKlUNsWRoLU8VbXEMN2HetdyFA&__tn__=-]K-R) to bring a set of **Tales of Darkness and Dread** at [Babushka Books](https://www.facebook.com/lovebabushkabooks?__cft__[0]=AZVjIhbtApaeu_LjG3EkFigyCBrlE_jWXar1b95SE7bC1wBQlZ2uPT0qk9Oo2B3LOrrWCZmjRY_N2BJ1ri8OcrS81F8Smlr2WFOLqlvI0pP3zQlxW5XR0NKBIPO6NkkAd9E_JPatuVSwBEBDOyPLnKlUNsWRoLU8VbXEMN2HetdyFA&__tn__=-]K-R) in Shanklin.

Bringing stories of the restless dead and vengeful spirits, and tales that will twist and turn and follow you home, doors open at 7pm for 7:30pm start on **Thursday 31st October** . Tickets **£7** including refreshments. Suitable for ages 16+

Booking essential. Phone **01983 652880** to book your place.
