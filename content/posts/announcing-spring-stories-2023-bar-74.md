With spring in the air, _'Tis Tales_ are pleased to announce a new collection of traditional tales — **Spring Stories**— presenting for the first time on the Ides of March — **Wednesday, March 15th, 2023, 7.15pm** for a 7.30pm start — at **Bar 74, Ryde**.

_Look forward to an enchanting evening of traditional spring tales, featuring gods and goddesses, flowers and fog, making it perfectly balanced between the darkness of winter and the lightness of summer._

Tickets are now available priced **£6** [in advance](https://www.ticketsource.co.uk/tis-tales/spring-stories/e-rkbavd) (includes booking fee), or on the door.
