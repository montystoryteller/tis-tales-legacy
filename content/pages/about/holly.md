An island based storyteller with a keen interest in darker Icelandic, Scandinavian and Eastern European tales, Holly delivers a wide range of fun and interactive activities for children focusing on heritage via [Unravelled Learning](https://www.facebook.com/unravelledlearning).

Holly also produces the _[History and Folklore Podcast](https://historyandfolklorepodcast.libsyn.com/)_ ( [@historyfolklore](https://twitter.com/historyfolklore)) which takes a look at folklore, through the lens of history, to help us understand people's perceptions of nature through time.
