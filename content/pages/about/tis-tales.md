Originally performing as _Three Island StoryTellers_, **'Tis Tales** is a performance storytelling group (Holly Medland, Tony "Monty" Hirst, Sue Bailey) from the Isle of Wight.

{{< figure src="/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/image-4.png" alt="" caption="" >}}

With a wide ranging repertoire of traditional folk tales, fairy tales, legend and myths taken from a wide range of traditions, **'Tis Tales** can provide a story for any occasion.

**For bookings, email: bookings@tistales.org.uk**

If you would like to join our mailing list, please email: **subscribe@tistales.org.uk**
