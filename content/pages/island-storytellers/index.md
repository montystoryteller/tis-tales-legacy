The _Island Storytellers_ is a friendly and open-invitation storytelling group that meets on the **second Wednesday** of every month alternating mornings (11-1; Town Choice café, Newport, April/June/Oct/Dec) and evenings (7-9, Yelfs Hotel, Ryde, May/July/Sept/Nov) for a casual story circle somewhere on the Isle of Wight.

{{< figure src="/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/image-3.png" alt="" caption="" >}}

**Tellers and listeners alike, all welcome.** Stories told, not read, and no longer than ~5 _-8_ minutes each, unless by arrangement.
